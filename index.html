Projects

  • Sequential Behavior Prediction. We seek to investigate sequential information and dynamic properties of users to enhance the performance of behavior prediction and recommendation. We have improved Recurrent Neural Networks (RNN), and achieved state-of-the-art performances in variety of situations. Our research achievements have some successful industrial applications: applied in iHealth for user modeling and healthcare; applied in CITIC Securities for user loss prediction and modeling user life circles on the financial platform. Several research papers are published in top conferences and journals such as AAAI, SIGIR, ICDM and TKDE.
  • Contextual Information Modeling. We seek to utilize variety of contextual information for better behavior modeling and recommendation. Our research is based on Matrix Factorization (MF) and Convolutional Neural Networks (CNN). Our method is the first one ever to incorporate CNN for modeling user behaviors. Our research achievements have been successfully applied in the online advertising system of Sina. Several research papers are published in top conferences and journals such as AAAI, CIKM and TKDE.
  • Network Information Credibility Evaluation. We seek to automatically evaluate credibility of information and detect misinformation on social media. We propose misinformation detection methods via learning dynamic representations, constructing attention models and mining user opinions. Based on our methods, an application system called NICE has been developed and applied. Research paper is published in AAAI.
  • User Personality Analysis. During the internship in MSRA, I work on the project of personality analysis on the XiaoIce chatbot. First, we infer the personality of a user based on avatars of the user, which has been applied in the XiaoIce chatbot. Second, we learn unsupervised personal conversation representations of users according to their conversation records, based on which personality inference can be performed. Research paper is submitted to KDD.
